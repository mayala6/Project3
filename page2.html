My Background

Family

Married to Denise for 6 years and have two kids, Andy and Julian. We live in the southside of Chicago in the Scottsdale neighborhood, which is near Burbank and Oak Lawn, Il. My son Andy is in prekinder at St. Germaine Grammar School. We also have dogs, Teddy and Lola, they're a mix bred, half maltese and half terrier. My wife is a school teacher at Zapata Academy, she teaches Social Studies to 7th graders.

Occupation

I am currently a police officer for Chicago Police Department. My assignment is in the 19th district which is the Wrigleyville neighborhood. I'm currenlty on bike patrol, which is great because it keeps me in great shape. Although I sweat a lot I feel it's rewarding. The scenery is awesome, our skyline is beautiful and I see it everyday. I also see a lot of boats and Belmont Harbor, Montrose Harbor and Diversey Harbor. Another reason I like my assigment is the lunch options are great, the food options are endless and there are no bad restaurants.
